Today's tips & tricks: YouTube Analytics

  • Notice performance trends - Always stay cautious and check your data when you realize slow progress or decreases in performance. Advise regarding the "ideal" content on YouTube should be taken with a grain of salt. Trends change easily, and it's poor to assume the strategies that have worked before will work again.
  • Identify what resonates with the viewers - Check for patterns and play close attention to view durations. YouTube Algorithm also take notes of the watch time of viewers. Those with higher watch time are more likely to be shown in the recommendations.
  • YouTube Search Engine Optimization - How users find your video and the search terms that they use are one of the most valuable data to figure out how more users can come across your videos. Look for target keywords, use captions, thumbnails, and hashtags. These assets could impact your video peformance. It is also important to assess how big these details of your content contribute to audience engagement.
